HOUSE RESOLUTION NO. 4 Reps. Posthumus and Fitzgerald offered the following resolution: A resolution to fix the hour for daily sessions. Resolved by the House of Representatives, That unless otherwise ordered, the daily sessions of the House of Representatives commence on Tuesday and Wednesday at 1:30 p.m. and on Thursday at 12:00 noon.
