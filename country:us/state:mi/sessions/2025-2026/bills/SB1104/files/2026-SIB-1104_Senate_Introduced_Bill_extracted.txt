SENATE BILL NO. 1104 SENATE BILL NO. 1104 A bill to amend 2016 PA 407, entitled "Skilled trades regulation act," (MCL 339.5101 to 339.6133) by adding section 206. the people of the state of michigan enact: Sec. 206. (1) A licensee may surrender a license issued under this article by delivering to the director the license and a written notice stating that the licensee surrenders the license. (2) The surrender of a license under this section does not affect the licensee's civil or criminal liability for acts committed before the surrender of the license. (3) The surrender of a license under this section does not impair or affect the obligation of any preexisting lawful contract between the licensee and a consumer.
