SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 9 Senator Singh offered the following concurrent resolution: A concurrent resolution to prescribe the legislative schedule. Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), That when the Senate adjourns on Thursday, November 13, 2025, it stands adjourned until Tuesday, December 2, 2025, at 10:00 a.m.; and be it further Resolved, That when the House of Representatives adjourns on Thursday, November 13, 2025, it stands adjourned until Tuesday, December 2, 2025, at 1:30 p.m.
