SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 1 Senator Singh offered the following resolution: A resolution notifying the Governor and the House of Representatives that the Senate is ready to proceed with the business of the session. Resolved by the Senate, That the Secretary of the Senate inform the Governor and the House of Representatives that a quorum of the Senate is present and that the Senate is ready to proceed with the business of the session.
